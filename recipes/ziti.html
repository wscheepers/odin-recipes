Baked Ziti

Home

Description

How to make a tasty baked Ziti in one hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26-ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1 ½ cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.
  3. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Spread half of the ziti in the bottom of the prepared dish.
  5. Top with provolone cheese.
  6. Spread sour cream over the cheese.
  7. Add half of the meat sauce, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese, and remaining meat sauce. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and cheeses have melted, about 30 minutes.
