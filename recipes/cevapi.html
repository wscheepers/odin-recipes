Description

How to make some appetizing cevapi in only 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 500 g mixed minced meat (beef and lamb)
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon paprika powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh flatbread (somun) for serving
  • Chopped onion and ajvar (pepper puree) as side dishes

Directions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the minced meat, onion, garlic, paprika, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small sausages, approximately the size of a finger.
  3. Preheat a grill pan or barbecue over medium heat. Grill the ćevapi for about 6-8 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  4. Serve the ćevapi on fresh flatbread, accompanied by chopped onions and ajvar.
